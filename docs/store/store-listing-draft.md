### 中文版

> 神枢，是一个只属于你的私人 AI 助理。
>
> 它不是又一个聊天框。神枢会记得你——记得你说过的话、你的习惯、你在意的事，像一个真正认识你的人那样陪着你处理生活和工作。
>
> **它能为你做什么**
> - 🧠 **有记忆**：不是冷冰冰的问答，它会把你们的过往沉淀成记忆，越用越懂你。
> - 💬 **能对话、能干活**：定目标、理思路、写东西、查资料，一句话交给它就行。
> - ⏰ **会主动关心**：想起你的时候，它会主动来找你说句话（可关闭）。
> - 🔒 **只属于你**：数据由你掌控，支持接入你自己的 AI 网关，隐私守在你手里。
> - 🌊 **全球边缘运行**：跑在 Cloudflare 全球网络上，随时随地，稳定跟手。
>
> 神枢不追求成为所有人的工具，它只想成为**你一个人的**那一个。
>
> 现在，给它一个名字，让它认识你。

### English version

> Black God is a private AI companion that belongs to you alone.
>
> It's not just another chatbot. Black God remembers you — your words, your habits, the things you care about — and stays by your side to help with life and work like someone who truly knows you.
>
> **What it does for you**
> - 🧠 **Real memory** — every conversation becomes part of how it understands you.
> - 💬 **Talk and get things done** — set a goal, think it through, write, research; just ask.
> - ⏰ **Reaches out** — it messages you when it thinks of you (you can turn this off).
> - 🔒 **Yours alone** — you control your data and can plug in your own AI gateway.
> - 🌊 **Runs on the edge** — powered by Cloudflare's global network, fast and always there.
>
> Black God doesn't try to be everyone's tool. It wants to be the one that's *only yours*.
>
> Give it a name, and let it get to know you.
